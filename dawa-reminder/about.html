Why Dawa Reminder Exists

Helping patients take the right medication, at the right time, every day.

Our Mission

Dawa Reminder was created to support patients under long-term treatment, especially those receiving care through AMPATH. Many patients miss doses due to busy schedules, forgetfulness, or lack of reminders.

Our mission is simple: reduce missed medication doses and improve treatment outcomes using an easy-to-use digital reminder system.
